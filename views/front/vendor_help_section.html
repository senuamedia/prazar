Paypal Integration

Prazar uses paypal for all payments to vendors when they make a sale on our platform. This means that all transactions are 100% safe and quick.

From here you can do everything from listing and managing products, printing invoices, adding tracking numbers, using our in built messaging system and much more.

Please update the below Paypal settings in order to receive payments from Prazar

1) Login to your paypal account and click on the settings symbol on the top right hand corner of the screen (this is next to the log out button).

2) From there, click on the last tab on the left hand side named “Selling tools” then click on the update button for “API access”.

3) In the first box “Pre-built payment solution” click on Grant API Permission. You will then be prompted to put in the “Third party permission username”, please copy and paste the below address and then press lookup;

payment_api1.prazar.com.au

You’ll then need to tick the following boxes and press save;

  1. Issue a refund for a specific transaction
  2. Authorise and capture your PayPal transactions
  3. Create and manage Recurring Payments
  4. Issue a refund for any prior transaction

Please note that you will not be able to receive payments without updating all of the above settings. You will not be charged transactions or any other fees by paypal, just the Prazar commission on sales.

Communicating with customers

All communication between Vendors and Customers is to be done via the Prazar inbuilt chat tool. You can find all of your messages under the Messages tab on your Vendor dashboard.

To contact a new customer simply head to the “Manage Orders” tab - then click “View order” - And finally click on the “Contact customer” button.

Alternatively if an order has already been marked as completed head to the “Order History” tab and follow the same process.

You will be able to find all of your existing conversations under the “Messages” tab on your dashboard.

When a new message has been received by a customer or a member of the Prazar team you will see a red dot appear above the “Messages” tab.

It is against Prazar policy to give out any contact, personal or business details such as email addresses, phone numbers or links to websites. All communication and transactions must be done via the messenger tool and Prazar web pages.

Promo codes

To view and manage promo codes go to the “Manage profile/shop” tab.

Promotional codes apply to your whole inventory and are used by customers at the checkout.

They can be created by inputting your own combination of a 10 capital letter sequence.

Promo codes can be turned on or off or edited at any time by using the manage promo codes section at the bottom of the “Manage profile/shop” tab.

Disclaimer; It is against Prazar policy to heavily increase retail prices of products and then add maximum percentage of either promo codes or product discounts.

Price of Shipping (Standard shipping within Australia)

When uploading or editing products you will be promoted to input the price of shipping. This will be the price charged to the customer for shipping anywhere in Australia. If you wish to offer free shipping simply enter 0.

Express Shipping Explained

It is entirely up to you the seller as to whether or not you offer express shipping on orders.

If you choose this as an option you will be prompted to input your own calculated express shipping cost.

The amount input will be the price charged to the customer for shipping anywhere in Australia.

Please note express shipping only applies to shipping within Australia

Shipping Overseas Explained

As with express shipping it is completely within the discretion of you as a seller as to whether or not you offer International shipping.

When uploading or editing a product you can opt to ship anywhere in the world or choose particular regions you would like to ship to.

You will then be prompted to input your own calculated shipping cost for the applicable regions.

Free shipping threshold explained

From the “Mange profiel/shop” tab you can define the free shipping threshold for your entire store.

Whatever value you decide to enter here will mean if a customer spends this exact amount or more on products from your store they will receive free shipping within Australia.

The free shipping threshold if activated will be shown on each individual product page under the “Delivery & Returns tab”.

Please make sure all orders go out with a printed invoice inside parcels and that tracking numbers and the name of the shipping company or link to their website are uploaded via the “Order Status” button as soon as they are available.

it is also against Prazar policy to put any promotional & advertising material, links to personal or business websites or any contact details inside packages that are sent out to customers.

Product Returns- Customer change of mind

While it’s rare that customers return products it is Prazar policy that all customers have up to 30 days from receipt of item(s) to make returns. You will receive email confirmation of any requested returns, please then head to the “Order History” tab and click on “View Order”.There will also be a red dot located on the tab and view order button which indicates that action is required. Once clicked through to the order you will see a button “Refund Request” please then click on this to view the return request made by the customer.

If you click the accept refund button, please then provide the customer with information for the next steps. For example if you wish the item to be returned then please provide the correct returns address and any other information they may require.

Once you have received the item(s) back and are happy to complete the refund click the “Refund status” button and then “process refund”. This will then automatically refund the customer for the total amount of the item purchased. Any shipping fees that were paid by the customer will not be refunded.

Please note that the customer is responsible for any postage costs relating to change of mind returns.

Personalised items, underwear, food & beverages, earrings, made to order items, or open bathroom or beauty products are non-refundable. All goods must also be returned by the original Customer.

It is also against Prazar policy for customers to exchange items.

You can view our full returns policy by heading to our website and clicking on the ‘Terms and Conditions” page (Clause 1.5)

Customers can opt for a refund as soon as vendors mark orders as “Complete” and will have 30 days from receipt of goods to do so.

Product Returns - Faulty or Damaged Products

For any return of faulty products this will also show as a “Return Requested” under the “Order history” tab. The customer should have already defined in the comments whether or not they require a refund or a new item to be sent out.

If they have requested a refund please proceed and process the full refund, the customer will then be refunded the full amount including any shipping charges they incurred upon delivery of items.

If the customer has requested a replacement item to be sent out please make sure to confirm this in the “next steps for customer” text box. You can then press the “cancel” button to cancel the payment refund. If you require the faulty item to be sent back then please make sure you send out a prepaid package, provide the customer with prepaid labels or reimburse them for any delivery charges.

You can contact customers at any time via the Prazar chat tool, and by clicking the “contact customer” button when viewing an order.

It is against Prazar policy for customers to exchange items.

You can view our full returns policy by heading to our ‘Terms and Conditions” page Customers can opt for a refund as soon as vendors mark orders as “Complete” and will have 30 days from receipt of goods to do so.

Cancelling orders

If you are unable to fulfill an order please head to the “Manage Orders” tab, click view order and then cancel order. The customer will then be automatically refunded.

Please also make sure you message the customer by using the “contact customer” button to explain why the order was cancelled.

Manage products

From the “Manage products” tab on your seller dashboard you will be able to add new products and edit existing inventory.

Whether you need to add more photos, change your product categories or simply update the availability of stock you’ll be able to do it all here.

Product Images

One of the most crucial aspects to a product listing.

We cannot stress enough how important it is to have good quality photos for your product listing.

Please use high resolution images and make sure that the first photo which will appear as the hero image is taken on a pure white background.

Images must fit within the below dimensions

Width 400 min X 1000 max PX and height 400 min X 1000 max PX

Prazar monitors all image uploads, and if we feel that certain photos are not up to our standards we will get in touch.

Product Descriptions

Another key element to any product listing, and where you really get to showcase your unique products.

In our experience listings with just a few words under the product description sell far less than those with well thought out text.

We suggest using somewhere between 150 to 200 words of descriptive text per product.

Please do not put any links to your website or contact details in product descriptions as it is against Prazar policy.

Product Variants

We recognise that many of our vendors products are not only unique but can be personalised in more than one way, and that’s why we allow every seller to add custom product variants to their listings.

To add custom variants to a product please click the variation option and then variants when uploading a new product, this will then take you to the full variants page.

Alternatively if you want to add or edit variants to existing inventory click on the “Mange products” tab, edit products and finally the edit or add variant button for the corresponding product.

If you can’t find what you’re looking for in the pre selected drop down menu of variants then just click any of the 5 custom variant slots and enter your unique information.

Custom labels

If your product has an option for custom writing or labels this can be added by clicking yes on the customisation option when uploading or editing a product. From here you can input a maximum of 5 labels and 50 characters per label. Once saved the customer will then have the option to input custom text into a box or boxes on the product listings page. You will also be able to define the price paid by the customer for custom text, however this will still show as an added option on the product listing.

This feature is ideal if you have products which can have custom text added to them.

Transaction History tab

From this tab you can view all successful transactions from fulfilled orders. The amount shown is the money received from each order minus the Prazar commission.

Order History tab

Once orders have been marked as complete by vendors they will automatically move from the “Manage Orders” tab to the “Order History” tab. If a refund or return request has been initiated by a customer you will see a red dot above the “Order history” tab.Once you have clicked through you will also see a red dot on the “view order” button for any orders which require action to be taken.

You can also contact customers and download invoices from this section.

Commission and payments Explained

As a platform Prazar was built to be completely transparent with its vendors and customers, and therefore we don’t believe in upfront or hidden charges.

To make sure the site runs as smoothly as possible and to help with any issues that may arise vendor or customer side we have a whole team of people working behind the scenes day and night.

We also have a clear and thought out advertising strategy to make sure we attract the right traffic to the site and get maximum coverage for our sellers.

Every time you make a sale on Prazar we take a 17% commission and no other fees or charges.

This means you can list as many products as you like and not have to worry about being charged if you don’t make a sale. Vendors are even paid into their PayPal accounts as soon as orders are marked as “Complete”.
